Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2025

This privacy policy explains how FOALCA ("we", "our", or "the project") collects and uses information related to visitors and users of our website and services.

1. What We Collect

  • Wallet address: When connecting your wallet to interact with the FOALCA platform, we temporarily store your address to enable reward distribution and track activity-based claims.
  • Browser data: Basic technical information like browser type, screen size, or referral source may be collected anonymously to help us improve the user experience.
  • Optional extension data: If you use the FOALCA Tracker extension, we may monitor website engagement points to calculate rewards — this data is never linked to your personal identity.

2. What We Don’t Do

  • We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not track users outside of our own ecosystem.

3. Use of Blockchain Data

All token interactions happen directly on-chain. We use wallet addresses solely to verify eligibility and execute reward distributions. No centralized KYC is required for basic users.

4. Investors & Strategic Partners

We may engage with select institutional or strategic partners who fund and help guide the project. These investors go through a separate legal process, including KYC/AML verification, as required by law.

5. Analytics & Improvement

We may use anonymized data for analytics, bug tracking, and performance enhancement. We use no invasive cookies, and most features work without storing any long-term data on our servers.

6. External Links

Our website may link to external websites or services (e.g. Discord, X.com). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those platforms.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us via our official communication channels listed on the homepage.
